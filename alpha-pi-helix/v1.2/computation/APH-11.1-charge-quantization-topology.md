## 1. Single Vortex Ring: Lepton Charge

The helical Compton vortex has fundamental circulation Γ = ħ/m_e. Electric charge is proportional to **winding number** w of the vortex filament:

Q = −w · e

Leptons (single ring): e⁻,μ⁻,τ⁻ have w=+1 → Q=−e. Neutrinos have w=0 → Q=0.

## 2. Triple-Linked Vortex: Quark Charge

Quarks are **triple-linked** vortex rings (Borromean type). Three linked rings share the total circulation: each ring carries Γ/3 = ħ/(3m_e). This gives fundamental quark charge unit:

q₀ = e/3

Each ring has independent winding wᵢ ∈ {−1, 0, +1}. Total charge:

Q = (w₁ + w₂ + w₃) · e/3

### Up-type (u, c, t): Q = +2/3
w = (+1, +1, 0) → Q = 2·e/3 ✓

### Down-type (d, s, b): Q = −1/3
w = (0, 0, −1) → Q = −e/3 ✓

### Colors: cyclic permutations
- Red: (+1,+1,0), Green: (0,+1,+1), Blue: (+1,0,+1)

### Anti-quarks: w → −w
ū: (−1,−1,0) → Q=−2/3 ✓, d̄: (0,0,+1) → Q=+1/3 ✓
