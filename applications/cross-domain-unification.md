### Limitations of the Vortex Model

1. **ZB reality:** The model's physical mechanism (physical ZB) is a minority interpretation. Foldy-Wouthuysen transformation eliminates ZB in QFT. The model is falsified if ZB is proven non-physical.

2. **No dynamics:** The vortex model describes *what* the electron is but not *how* vortices evolve. There is no vortex Lagrangian or Hamiltonian.

3. **Single-particle:** Only the electron is modeled. Extensions to muon, tau, quarks, and neutrinos are aspirational.

4. **No quantum field theory:** The model is classical-geometric. Quantization of the vortex is an open problem.

5. **No gravity derivation:** "Gravity is emergent" is claimed but not derived. The Jacobson-Sakharov program is cited as precedent, not as evidence.

6. **Mass scales unexplained:** The absolute values of $m_e$, $m_\mu$, $m_\tau$ are not predicted — only their ratios are noted as potentially topological.
