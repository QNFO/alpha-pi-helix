## Executive Summary

**The α-π-Helix Phase 11 Particle Zoo claims have significant structural overlap with the Bilson-Thompson helon model (2005-2008), which was developed 18-21 years earlier in the context of Loop Quantum Gravity and published in peer-reviewed journals.** The overlap is strongest in the areas of charge quantization from topology (both derive ±e/3), color SU(3) from 3-strand topology (both use 3-way topological structures), and generation structure from braid/knot complexity. The α-π-Helix project does not cite Bilson-Thompson anywhere in its 50,000-character paper, WBS, computation files, or gap analysis.

**Priority determination:** The helon model has PRIORITY on all overlapping claims by 18-21 years. The α-π-Helix Phase 11 adds mass derivations (n²×p — falsified in P1 tests) and PMNS/CKM mixing (w=0, +1 — undefined parameter). These additions are the weakest and least credible parts of Phase 11.

**Recommendation:** Any publication of α-π-Helix Phase 11 claims MUST cite Bilson-Thompson (2005) as prior art and explicitly distinguish what is new (if anything). The overlapping claims should be framed as "independent confirmation of the Bilson-Thompson braid framework using different mathematical tools (knot theory vs. braid group)" rather than "novel derivations."

## 3. Critical Differences

### 3.1 What Bilson-Thompson Does Better

1. **Mathematical rigor:** Chester et al. (2025) proved the helon model maps to the SU(3)_c × U(1)_em weight lattice of the Standard Model. Phase 11 has no comparable rigor proof.

2. **Gauge bosons:** The helon model classifies W⁺, Z⁰, γ as braids. Phase 11 has no boson classification (identified as a gap in the falsification planner).

3. **Peer review:** Published in Class. Quant. Grav. and J. Math. Phys. Phase 11 is unpublished and internally contradictory.

4. **CPT invariance:** Chester et al. (2025) proved the helon model is self-consistent with CPT. Phase 11 has no such proof.

5. **Generational honesty:** The helon model admits generations are "unbounded" — it does not claim to force exactly 3. Phase 11 claims exactly 3 but the argument is post-hoc.

### 3.2 What α-π-Helix Attempts That Bilson-Thompson Doesn't

1. **Mass derivation:** n²×p is a genuinely novel attempt, but it has been falsified.

2. **Experimental signatures:** The α-π-Helix WBS proposes specific experiments (channeling EDM, storage ring THz). Bilson-Thompson focuses on consistency with known data.

3. **Constant unification:** The α-π-Helix core (v1.1) provides a geometric reinterpretation of α, π, and mass that is independent of the particle zoo claims.

### 3.3 What α-π-Helix Could Genuinely Add (If Rigorized)

1. **A bridge between Compton-scale vortex and braid topology:** If the Dirac Zitterbewegung helical motion can be mapped to braid group B₃ representations, this would connect the Bilson-Thompson model to standard QFT — a significant theoretical advance.

2. **A specific generation-count argument:** If the (p,2) restriction can be justified from physical principles (not just post-hoc), this would go beyond Bilson-Thompson's "unbounded" result.

3. **Experimental predictions:** The channeling and storage ring proposals are concrete and testable, going beyond the helon model's kinematic focus.
