### Critical Finding: The Koide Fixed-Point Does NOT Exist

- Pole-mass Q = 0.6666605 (9.2 ppm from 2/3)
- QED running monotonically DECREASES Q → Q(μ) moves AWAY from 2/3 at all physical scales
- **No fixed-point exists** — Q never crosses 2/3
- Vortex mass matrix H_ij = (A−B)n_i²δ_ij + B·n_i·n_j with r_K = 0.934827 gives Q = 2/3 exactly
- **BUT:** eigenvalues for n = (1,2,3) give m_μ = 2.1 MeV (−98%) and m_τ = 78 MeV (−96%)
- Correct Koide structure, wrong mass hierarchy — winding numbers (1,2,3) are incorrect
- Correct parameterization: √(m) ∝ \|w\| (braid length), not n (winding number)
